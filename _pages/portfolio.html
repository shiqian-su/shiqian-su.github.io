TaCOS: Comprehensive Management System for Student Organizations

Solo Developer | Full-Stack System Published on Oct 2025 and still being updated.
TaCOS Website(But only accessible for THU students)

TaCOS is a comprehensive full-stack system I independently developed from scratch to support student organizations and art groups at Tsinghua University. The system provides end-to-end solutions for information management, asset tracking, team organization, and day-to-day operations, significantly improving the efficiency and effectiveness of student organization management. This system has now been fully adopted by Tsinghua University Student Art Troupe Choir in their daily operations.
